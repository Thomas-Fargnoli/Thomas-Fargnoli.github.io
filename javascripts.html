Thomas's Bitmoji Bitmoji Dancing The Race
This is a custom bitmoji that I coded based on a picture of myself. I had to program my glass, headwear, and any decals on my shirt. This is a spin off of my bitmoji in which I had to make the bitmoji appear at random points everytime the program is restarted. Further spinning off my bitmoji program, I had to make it travel across the screen at a random speed. I then had to take the Khan academy programs for a bunny and frog, and make them do the same as my bitmoji. They are suppose to appear to race and the result will be different everytime.
